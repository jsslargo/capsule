**Innovations covered by this application:**

1. Six-section atomic record structure (Trigger, Context, Reasoning, Authority, Execution, Outcome)
2. Pre-execution reasoning capture — reasoning recorded before action execution, not reconstructed afterward

4. Dual cryptographic signatures (Ed25519 + ML-DSA-65) on every record

6. Mandatory capture architecture where audit records cannot be bypassed
